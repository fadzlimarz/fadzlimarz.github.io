History

Founded in 2020, Hayabusa has been a pioneer in sustainable transportation. Our journey began with a vision to create eco-friendly EVs that reduce the carbon footprint and make the world a cleaner place. Over the years, we’ve achieved numerous milestones and contributed to the EV revolution.

Our Mission

Our mission is to lead the transition to sustainable and eco-friendly transportation by providing high-quality EVs. We aim to make EVs accessible to everyone, and together with our partners, we strive to reduce the world’s dependence on fossil fuels.

Our Values

Hayabusa comes from the Japanese word for “peregrine falcon”. Similar to this swift bird of prey, our EVs embody speed, agility, and a dedication to eco-friendly transportation. This name symbolizes the fusion of Japanese culture with our pursuit of efficient and environmentally conscious mobility.

  • Sustainability: We are committed to preserving the environment.
  • Innovation: We constantly seek innovative solutions.
  • Quality: We prioritize the quality of our products and services.
  • Customer-Centric: We value our customers’ needs and satisfaction.
